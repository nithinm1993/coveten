Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services

What industries do you serve?

We serve six major industries: Aviation, Automobile, Electronics, Electrical, Robotics, and Industrial Safety. Our expertise spans across these sectors with specialized products and services tailored to each industry's requirements.

Do you provide international shipping?

Yes, we provide global shipping services to customers worldwide. We work with reliable logistics partners to ensure safe and timely delivery of your orders. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on location and product specifications.

What is your typical response time for quotes?

We typically respond to quote requests within 24 hours during business days. For complex projects requiring detailed analysis, it may take up to 48-72 hours. We'll always acknowledge your request immediately and provide an expected timeline.

Do you offer technical support and consultation?

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive technical support and engineering consultation services. Our expert team can help with project planning, design, material selection, and implementation. We offer both pre-sales and post-sales support.

Can you source custom or specialized components?

Yes, we have an extensive global supplier network that allows us to source custom and specialized components. If we don't have what you need in our standard inventory, we can help you find and procure the right components for your specific requirements.

What quality certifications do your products have?

Our products meet international quality standards and industry-specific certifications including ISO, FAA (for aviation), CE marking, and others relevant to each industry. All products come with proper documentation and certificates of compliance.
